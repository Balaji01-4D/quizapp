Welcome to the Algorithm Quiz! Here's how you can play and maximize your score:

  • Register with your name and registration number to begin.
  • After registration, click Start to begin the quiz round.
  • Each question will appear one at a time with multiple options.
  • Read carefully and select the correct answer before the timer runs out (1 minute per question).
  • Once you select an option, you can change it before moving to the next question.
  • Click Next to proceed. If you don't answer in time, it will be marked as unanswered.
  • After the last question, your answers are submitted automatically.
  • Points are awarded for correct answers. Faster answers may boost your ranking!
  • Check your Leaderboard rank after submitting the quiz.
Tips to Win Effectively:
  • Stay calm and read each question fully before answering.
  • Don't rush, but keep an eye on the timer.
  • Accuracy matters more than speed, but quick correct answers help your rank.
  • Use a stable internet connection to avoid interruptions.
